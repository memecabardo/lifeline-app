Discover LifeLine: Your Disaster Management Solution

Join us in preparing for the unexpected with Lifeline, the ultimate disaster management app that equips you with the tools and knowledge to stay safe and secure in any emergency situation.

Why Lifeline is Essential?

Empower yourself with Lifeline. It provides essential information and resources to help you navigate through crises, ensuring that you and your loved ones are well-prepared and protected.

LifeLine Features

Real-time Updates

Stay informed with Lifeline’s real-time updates on weather conditions, disaster notifications, and evacuation center locations to ensure you’re prepared in any situation. This feature also includes family location tracking, allowing you to know the real-time whereabouts of your loved ones during emergencies. Lifeline keeps you connected, prepared, and ready to act when it matters most.

Learn More

Quick Help Connection

Lifeline’s Quick Help Connections provide instant access to essential emergency contacts, including your family, PNP, BFP, and 911. In times of crisis, every second counts, and with Lifeline, you can quickly reach out to the right people for immediate support. Stay prepared and connected, knowing that help is always just a tap away when you need it most.

Learn More

Essential Packing Suggestion

Lifeline provides practical packing suggestions to ensure you're ready for any emergency. Whether you're evacuating or preparing for a natural disaster, the app guides you on what to bring—such as important documents, first aid kits, food, water, and other essentials. With Lifeline's packing recommendations, you can stay organized and ensure you have everything you need to stay safe during a crisis.
