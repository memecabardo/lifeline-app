Quick Help Connection

Lifeline’s Quick Help Connections feature ensures you’re always prepared with immediate access to essential emergency contacts. In times of crisis, knowing where to find help instantly is vital, and Quick Help Connections offers just that—bringing together critical contacts in one place for fast response.

The Family Contacts feature allows you to store the numbers of family members, ensuring they’re only a tap away when you need them most. Whether it's checking in on loved ones or coordinating emergency plans, reaching family quickly is invaluable.

Additionally, Direct Contact with PNP (Philippine National Police) provides users with a fast way to connect with law enforcement in emergencies, such as accidents or security threats. This feature helps ensure you can get the immediate assistance you need from trained authorities. The BFP (Bureau of Fire Protection) contact lets you quickly reach fire services in case of fire emergencies, providing peace of mind and faster response time. Finally, 911 Access ensures that any immediate need, from medical to rescue services, is quickly accessible through a single tap.

Quick Help Connections is a streamlined solution, organizing all essential contacts in one place, so that in any situation, you’re never without support.
